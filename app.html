Features

  • An activist's feed

    Bring all your favorite organisations together in your own activism feed.

  • Easy to read

    Avoid noise from topics not related to social activism and stay focused on what matters.

  • What's going on

    Find all the events going on near you and never miss a protest, workshop or more!

  • Handy links

    No more searching where to donate to or educate yourself about your favorite activism topic.

  • All-in-One

    Keep track of all the new posts from various social media pages in one place.

  • Personal agenda

    Save the events that interest you in an easy-thread calendar and never forget about your activism agenda.

  • Share the news

    Share your favorite posts and events to other social media to get the word out.

  • All the info

    Find information about your favorite activism organisation on their in-app profile.
